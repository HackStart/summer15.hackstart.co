About

So what exactly is HackStart?

HackStart is a hackathon for high school students. Over the course of three days and two nights, you will face new challenges, meet new people, and build something extraordinary. Form teams of four to develop apps and pitch them to judges for a unique opportunity to take home a cash prize!

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can't code?

The purpose of this event is for you to learn something new; if you can't code, this is the perfect opportunity for you to learn. We will offer programming workshops to get you started, but keep in mind that any creative non-technical entries still have a shot at winning.

When and where will this be happening?

HackStart will be happening early July in downtown Atlanta. More details will be provided later.

How much does it cost to attend?

HackStart is completely free. We will pay for your meals and any related costs, so there is no price on your part. Just show up!

What if I don't have a team?

The team registration period will be happening on the first day of the event. During this time we will provide you with opportunities to meet other people and form new dynamic teams.

What can I build?

You can build anything you want! Whether it's a website, an mobile app, or a computer app, as long as it was created in the 48 hours of the event, it's perfectly OK! Be creative with what you decide to build!

What if I have never attended a Hackathon before?

That's good! No previous experience is required to have a good time. This is a great opportunity for you to meet new people and learn new concepts that you wouldn't even think of learning.
